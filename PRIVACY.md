Privacy Policy – TennisSmart

Last Updated: [08 March 2026]

TennisSmart (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is a tennis performance tracking and analysis application. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the TennisSmart mobile application.

By using TennisSmart, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

TennisSmart may collect the following types of information to provide match tracking and performance insights.

A. Match and Performance Data

When you record matches in TennisSmart, the app may collect and store:

• Match results (win/loss)
• Scores
• Point-by-point tracking data
• Shot types and rally patterns
• Tactical intent selections
• Performance statistics (serve %, return %, rally data, etc.)
• Match surface and conditions

This information is used to generate analytics and coaching insights.

B. Location Data (Optional)

TennisSmart may request permission to access your device location.

Location data is used only to:

• Retrieve weather conditions for matches
• Provide performance analysis based on environmental conditions

Location access is optional and can be disabled at any time in your device settings.

TennisSmart does not continuously track your location.

C. Device Information

We may collect limited technical information including:

• Device type
• Operating system version
• App version

This information helps improve compatibility and diagnose technical issues.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected by TennisSmart is used to:

• Record and analyze tennis matches
• Generate AI-based performance insights
• Provide personalized coaching recommendations
• Improve application performance and features
• Fix bugs and technical issues

Your data is not used for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Match data and analytics are stored:

• On your device
• Or on secure cloud infrastructure (if cloud syncing is enabled)

We take reasonable security measures to protect your data.

4. Data Sharing

TennisSmart does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising.

Some services may be used strictly for app functionality, such as:

• Cloud storage
• Weather data providers
• Analytics tools

These services only process data necessary to operate the app.

5. AI Insights

TennisSmart may generate automated insights based on your match statistics and performance data.

These insights:

• Are generated algorithmically
• Are intended for informational and training purposes only
• Do not replace professional coaching advice

6. Your Privacy Choices

You can control your data in several ways:

• Disable location permissions in device settings
• Stop recording matches in the app
• Contact us to request data deletion (if cloud storage is used)

To request deletion of your data, contact us at the email below.

7. Data Retention

We retain match and performance data for as long as you continue to use TennisSmart.

If you request deletion of your account or data, we will remove your information within a reasonable timeframe, subject to technical limitations.

8. Children’s Privacy

TennisSmart is not specifically intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

9. Security

We implement reasonable security measures designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If we make significant changes, the updated policy will be posted with a revised “Last Updated” date.

Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
